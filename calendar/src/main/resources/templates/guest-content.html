About Cactus Calendar:

Cactus Calendar is really helpfull site for cactus plantators. The site use weather api, and custom algoritm for check if weather conditions are good for planting your cactuses. The main feature of the app is the weather calendar.

Additionally, there is also a scheduling feature so you don't miss a thing.

Also you can choose your region trough your city(Only Polish cities).
